About Me

I am Gabriel, a Research Associate at the Visual Cognitive Neuroscience Lab, Nanyang Technological University. I earned both my Bachelor’s (Honours with Highest Distinction) and Master’s degrees from the National University of Singapore through its Concurrent Degree Programme. With seven years of research experience in Social, Cognitive, and Educational Psychology, I am proficient in using advanced statistical methods to analyse complex datasets.
