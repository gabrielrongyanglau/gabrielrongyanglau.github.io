About Me

I am Gabriel, a Research Associate at the Visual Cognitive Neuroscience Lab, Nanyang Technological University. I hold both a B.Soc.Sci. (Hons. with Highest Distinction) and an M.Soc.Sci. from the National University of Singapore, conferred through its highly selective Concurrent Degree Programme. During my candidature, I achieved top academic honours, including placement on the Dean’s Scholars List (Top 1%), Dean’s List (Top 5%), and receipt of the Dean’s Commendation Letter. I was also awarded the prestigious US-Singapore Summer Exchange Scholarship by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, through which I undertook summer programmes at Harvard University and Yale University. Beyond academics, I was honoured as the university’s top student leader, receiving the Leadership Award (Distinction), Campus Choice Award, and twice earning the Colours Award. With seven years of research experience, I am proficient in using advanced statistical methods to analyse complex datasets. My current research interest lies in leveraging AI to advance understanding in the fields of Social, Cognitive, and Health Psychology.
